Brief Synopsis:

In the sleepy and idyllic Shire, a young hobbit is given a task: to guard the One Ring and embark on the journey to its destruction in "The Cracks Of Fate". Accompanied by wizards, men, elves and dwarves, he will cross Middle Earth and enter the shadows of Mordor, always pursued by the hosts of Sauron, the dark lord, ready to recover his creation to establish the definitive domain of evil.
